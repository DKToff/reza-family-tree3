For the Generations After Us


This website exists to document and preserve our Afghan family history.

Many of our ancestors lived through difficult times, yet their stories shaped who we are today.

By creating this website, we ensure that their names are remembered, their journeys are respected, and their legacy continues through future generations of our Afghan family.

This family tree is our way of honoring them.
